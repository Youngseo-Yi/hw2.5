About the Instructor

Youngseo Yi is a third-degree black belt who currently trains at CW Taekwondo in Boston, MA. Youngseo started her Taekwondo journey at Musa Taekwondo in Bedford, MA (formerly known as Youn's Taekwondo) before joining CW in 2017. She currently competes at a regional and national level with multiple accolades, including bronze in the 2023 USATKD National Championships sparring -67kg division.

Youngseo is primarily trained in the World Taekwondo branch, with multiple years of experience in poomsae and sparring. She currently teaches youth classes at CW Taekwondo for ages 5-15, and has experience in teaching youth and adult Taekwondo classes.
