About the Class

Each class is a 1-2 hour online Zoom session with primary focus on the goals and objectives of the student. There are three formats that classes can take:

  • Structured, following our online curriculum with focused training in forms and kicking techniques
  • Flexible, with the student specifying areas of improvement during the class to work on throughout the session
  • Dialogue, with the student conversing with the instructor for coaching or advice. This class does not involve active Taekwondo training during the class, but serves as a means to help the student identify their current skills, goals, and motivations in Taekwondo.

Classes are scheduled ahead of time, based on availability for the student and the instructor. Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours prior to the lesson.

The student is responsible for finding an adequate space to train safely. A flat 3x3 meter space with no obstacles or obstructions is recommended for active Taekwondo training. Resistance bands, dumbbells, or additional training tools are ideal for more advanced training, but not required.
